How to Request Deletion

Option 1 — Through your College Admin
All student and faculty data can be deleted directly by your institution's College Admin within the DCampus AI Portal. If your institution manages its own accounts, please contact your College Admin to have your account and associated data removed.

Option 2 — Email Request

  1. Send an email to dkappz@outlook.com.
  2. Mention your registered institutional email and role.
  3. Use the subject: DCampus AI Portal - Delete My Account.
  4. We may verify your identity before processing.
