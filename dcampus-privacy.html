Privacy Policy — DCampus AI Portal

Last updated: June 2026

DCampus AI Portal ("the App") is an institutional attendance and leave management application, powered by CampusLogic AI™, developed under the dkapps brand by LaunchGrid. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, institutional email address, phone number, role (student/faculty/staff), and department, provided during sign-up or by your institution's administrator.
  • Attendance data: daily attendance records, check-in/check-out timestamps, and class/session details.
  • Leave records: leave applications, approval status, and related remarks submitted through the app.
  • Device information: basic device data (model, OS version) for app performance and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Information

  • To record and manage attendance for your institution.
  • To process and route leave applications to the relevant approver.
  • To power CampusLogic AI™ features such as attendance insights and reporting for institutional administrators.
  • To maintain and improve app stability and performance.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud infrastructure) with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted by role-based authentication — students, faculty, and administrators see only the data relevant to their role.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is shared only:

  • With your institution's authorised administrators and faculty, as required for attendance and leave management.
  • With service providers (e.g. Firebase/Google Cloud) strictly to operate the app.
  • If required by law or institutional policy.

Data Retention

Attendance and leave records are retained for the duration required by your institution's academic record-keeping policies. Account data is removed upon request or when access is revoked by your institution.

Your Rights

You may request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting your institution's administrator or by reaching out to us directly using the details below.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use within educational institutions by students, faculty, and staff. It is not designed for use by children under 13 outside an institutional context.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, email hello@launchgrid.in or WhatsApp us via the LaunchGrid website.
