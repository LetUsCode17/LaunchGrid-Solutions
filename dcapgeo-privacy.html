1. Overview

DCapGeo ("the App", "we", "us") is developed and published by LaunchGrid (dkapps brand), based in Tamil Nadu, India. DCapGeo is a high-precision forensic imaging tool that captures photos embedded with verified GPS coordinates, timestamps, altitude, bearing, and AI-classified scene labels — for use in field surveys, legal documentation, construction records, and similar professional applications.

We are committed to handling your personal data responsibly and transparently, in accordance with applicable data protection principles. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of DCapGeo on Android devices.

2. Data We Collect

The table below lists every category of data DCapGeo collects, with the reason each is needed.

Data Type What is collected Why it is needed
Account data Name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account (via Google Sign-In) To authenticate you securely and identify your session
Precise location GPS latitude, longitude, altitude (MSL), and compass bearing at the exact moment of each photo capture Core feature — permanently embeds verified location data as a forensic watermark on every captured image
Photos / images Images captured via the in-app camera at maximum hardware sensor resolution Primary output of the app — stored with embedded forensic metadata
Timestamps Full GMT date and time (to the millisecond) at moment of capture Embedded in forensic watermark for legal and audit traceability
AI scene classification Scene/environment label generated by on-device Google ML Kit at capture (e.g. "Construction", "Infrastructure") Provides automated professional categorisation of evidence; processed on-device, not sent to external servers
Device installation record A unique device identifier logged to Firebase Firestore upon first app launch Enterprise-level installation tracking for app distribution management
Device information Device model, Android OS version, display refresh rate, camera and GPS capability flags App performance optimisation, crash reporting, and compatibility checks
⚠️
Important — location is captured at shutter press only. DCapGeo accesses your GPS at the moment you take a photo to embed verified coordinates into that image. The app does not track or log your location continuously in the background.
✅
AI processing is on-device. Scene classification using Google ML Kit runs entirely on your device. No images are sent to an external AI server for analysis.

3. Device Permissions Required

DCapGeo requests the following Android permissions. Each is required for specific features:

  • Camera — to capture forensic images using the device's maximum hardware resolution via CameraX.
  • Fine Location (GPS) — to record precise latitude, longitude, altitude, and bearing at the moment of capture for the forensic watermark.
  • Write External Storage / Media — to save high-resolution images to the dedicated Pictures/DCapGeo folder and register them with the Android Media Scanner.
  • Internet — to authenticate via Google Sign-In and to log device installation records to Firebase Firestore.
ℹ️
You may deny or revoke permissions at any time via your device's Settings. Revoking Camera or Location permissions will disable the core forensic capture functionality. Revoking Storage permission will prevent images from being saved to your gallery.

4. Third-Party Services

DCapGeo uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy which governs how they handle data.

  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — used for secure user authentication. Google may collect data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Firestore (Google LLC) — used to log unique device installation records for enterprise distribution tracking. Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Google ML Kit (Google LLC) — used for on-device AI scene classification at the moment of capture. Image data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to Google's servers for this feature.
  • CameraX (Google LLC / Jetpack) — Android camera framework used to access the device's hardware camera sensor. No data is sent externally by CameraX itself.

5. Data Storage & Security

Local storage: All captured forensic images are saved locally on your device to a dedicated Pictures/DCapGeo directory. These files remain on your device and are under your control.

Cloud storage: Only your Google account profile (used for authentication) and your device installation record are stored remotely — in Firebase Firestore on Google Cloud infrastructure, with encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Security: Access to your account data is protected by Google Sign-In authentication. We apply industry-standard security practices and rely on Google Cloud's security infrastructure for all remotely stored data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data or your forensic images to any third party. Data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Google — for authentication (Google Sign-In) and cloud infrastructure (Firebase), as described in Section 4.
  • When you choose to share — when you use the app's share or export feature to send images or collage reports to another person or application, you control who receives that data.
  • Legal obligation — if required by applicable law, court order, or government authority.
✅
Your forensic images stay on your device. DCapGeo does not upload your captured photos to any server operated by LaunchGrid or any third party, except when you explicitly export or share them yourself.

7. Data Retention

Forensic images: Stored locally on your device indefinitely until you delete them manually within the app or via your device's gallery.

Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleting your account removes your authentication record from Firebase.

Device installation record: The Firestore installation log entry is retained for as long as the app is installed. You may request its deletion at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account, authentication record, and device installation log from our systems.
  • Permission revocation: Revoke Camera, Location, or Storage permissions at any time via your device settings.
  • Data portability: Your forensic images are already stored locally on your device in standard image formats — they are fully portable without any action from us.

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

9. Children's Privacy

DCapGeo is a professional forensic tool intended for adult field professionals, surveyors, legal professionals, and construction teams. It is not directed at or designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app and provided personal data, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

Continued use of DCapGeo after an updated policy is posted constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
